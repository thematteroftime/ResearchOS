# Quantum Chaos in Driven Spin Systems - Summary

## Key findings (from macrospin and mean-field papers)
- **Lyapunov exponent (λ_max)**: Criterion for quantum-classical convergence; λ_max < 0 indicates stable classical orbits.
- **Bifurcation structure**: Maps chaotic, quasiperiodic, and periodic phases via MLE and Fourier spectra.
- **Quantum chaos**: Density matrix delocalization, Hilbert-space diffusion beyond Lyapunov time.
- **Simulated vs quantum reverse annealing**: SRA can avoid discontinuous phase transitions in wider parameter regime than ARA.

## Methodology
- Lindblad master equation, mean-field limit, Dicke basis for finite N.
- Free energy landscape, p-spin model, adiabatic reverse annealing protocols.
